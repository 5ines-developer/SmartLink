ABOUT SMART LINK

Incubated in the City of Gold, Dubai, UAE, Smart Link Telecommunications Trading L.L.C provides exemplary telecom solutions through innovation, technical expertise and fair business practices. Smart Link, as the name suggests—with defined, designed technology and process enabled business solutions, aid our clients to grow up to their prospects—ushering in value, reputation and connections; all the smartest way.

Who We Are & What We Do

Managing telecom expenses require a special blend of knowledge of IT, telecom services, corporate finance, and service providers' billing procedures and their internal updates. It’s always a task to meet your requirements complying telecom and the latest technology within your budget, especially for SME’s.

We understand and value our clients’ increasing demand in the telecom sector and concern about their financial expenditure with an unparalleled commitment.
